About Us

BORN TO
DRIVE

Apex Drive was founded by a group of passionate drivers who believed that the greatest roads in the world deserve to be driven properly — in the right cars, with the right people, at the right pace.

We meticulously plan every route, every stop, and every detail to ensure that from the first ignition to the final handshake, the experience is one you'll talk about for years.

  • Founded in 2018 by drivers, for drivers
  • Events across the UK and continental Europe
  • Maximum 20 cars per event — always exclusive
  • Full insurance and breakdown coverage included
  • All experience levels welcome
